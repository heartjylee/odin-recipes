Match Latte

Description

Ingredients
  • Matcha Powder 1tsp
  • Milk 3/4 Cups
  • Maple Syrup 1Tsp
Steps
  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Sift the matcha powder into a mug.
  3. Add the hot water and whisk until no lumps remain. Stir in the Maple syrup.
  4. Add the milk and either whisk to combine or use a milk frother; serve immediately.
